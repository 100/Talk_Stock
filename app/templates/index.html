An environment for stock predictions.






Powered by Reddit

Reddit will be as a platform where recent events and news articles will be posted on the subreddit /r/talkstock. Reddit will be used as a tool for maintaining the categorization of quality news events that will have a relevance to affecting certain stocks.

NY Times API

We are using the NY Times API so that we will be able to retrieve a reliable source of news related to top businesses or other news events that may play a role in affecting the stock market. Articles will span the lenght of about seven days to generally map the current trend of various stocks.

(Feature is not implemented) We will be using Bloomberg's data service to provide information that will prove to be useful in predictions as well as demonstrating its role in the results of stocks.
